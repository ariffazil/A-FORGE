# 1️⃣ What AGI Actually Means

1. **Generalization across domains** (not just one task)

6. **Autonomous planning over long horizons**

Most current systems (including LLMs) are:
- Very strong pattern reasoners
- Weak persistent agents
- Not self-grounded
- Not autonomously goal-stable
